# MuleShield AI

AI-powered mule account prevention platform for fintech and banking systems.

## Problem

MuleShield AI focuses on detecting and preventing mule account activities before financial crimes happen.

Many people, especially young adults, may rent or share their bank accounts without understanding the legal and financial risks. These accounts can later be used for fraud, illegal betting, money laundering, or suspicious money transfers.

## Solution

MuleShield AI analyzes transaction patterns, account behavior, and risk signals to identify suspicious account activity.

The platform provides:

- Risk scoring
- Mule account behavior detection
- Explainable AI alerts
- Bank dashboard
- Early warning system
